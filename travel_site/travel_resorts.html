This page is all about the incredible ski and snowboard resorts in Utah.

We'll focus on the following resorts:
  • Brighton Resort

  • Snowbird, Utah

  • Solitude Mountain Resort

Brighton Resort

Brighton Resort is known for it's "Ski and Snowboard School". The mountain averages 500 in. of snow each winter, and it's great condition too. One hundered percent of Brighton's Terrain can be accessed by high speed quads. Brighton Resort also has terrain parks and includes night riding!

Snowbird

 Snowbird Resort is 2,500 acres, and has 140 runs. Little Cottonwood Canyon, where the resort is located, is known for it's powdery snow worldwide. They also have a Mountain School and terrain park. Their website reports daily weather conditions in and around the resort. 

Solitude

Solitude Resort contains 1,200 skiable acres, with 82 named runs. The resort averages 500 in. of snowfall per year. Their website also contains a weather and snow forecast.
